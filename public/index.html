Buying and Selling Celtic Coin

We’re not on any exchanges (yet) so currently it’s direct purchase only. On the plus side, that means there’s no exchange fee/commission to pay. The downside, if you can call it that, is this involves an element of trust as you pay for the coin first and we send it to your wallet. The beauty of using Crypto is that it’s all auditable (if you want it to be).

Step-by-step
- Get yourself an Ethereum ready wallet
- Send payment with a note of wallet address
- When payment verified your wallet will be credited with Celtic Coin (CFC)
- View transaction on Etherscan (https://etherscan.io/)
